Ramzan Prayer & Hadees does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data remains locally on your device.

Location Permission

The app requests location access for one purpose only:

  • To calculate accurate prayer times based on your current location
  • To determine Qibla direction using your device's compass

Important: Location data is processed entirely on your device. It is NEVER sent to any server, cloud, or third party. You can deny location permission and still use manual location entry.

Qibla Compass

The 3D Qibla compass uses your device's magnetometer and accelerometer sensors. This data is processed locally and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Hadith Library

The Hadith collection is stored locally within the app. No internet connection is required to read Hadiths. We do not track which Hadiths you read or how much time you spend in the app.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, children can use the app with complete privacy.

Data Security

Because we collect no data, there is no risk of data breach. All app functionality works offline. Your privacy is 100% protected by design.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever need to update this privacy policy, we will post the new version here. However, our commitment to zero data collection will never change.
