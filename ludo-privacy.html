Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy describes how Ludo Master Elite ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a competitive gaming experience, we collect the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Google Display Name, Email Address, and Profile Picture (via Google Sign-In).
  • Device & Technical Data: IP Address, Hardware model, OS version, unique device identifiers (Android ID/AD-ID), and crash logs.
  • Gameplay Records: Match wins/losses, coin balances, level progress, and global leaderboard rankings.
  • Age Data: We collect your self-reported age to ensure compliance with global child safety regulations.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We take children's safety seriously. Ludo Master Elite implements an Age Gate:

  • Users under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction) will have social features, custom chat, and personalized advertising automatically disabled.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal identifiers from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Matchmaking: To pair you with opponents of similar skill levels.
  • Leaderboards: To display your ranking among global players.
  • Cloud Sync: Using Firebase to ensure your progress is saved across devices.
  • Security: To detect and ban hackers or players using unauthorized scripts/mods.

4. Third-Party Services & Integration

We use industry-standard services that may collect data independently:

  • Google Play Games: Handles authentication and cloud saves.
  • Firebase (Google): Real-time database, Analytics, and Crashlytics.
  • AdMob: Serves advertisements. You can reset your Advertising ID in your Android device settings.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You have the Right to be Forgotten:

  • You can request account deletion via the in-game settings.
  • Once requested, all personal records, match history, and email links will be permanently wiped from our Firebase servers within 30 days.

6. Data Security

All data is transmitted via SSL/TLS encryption. While we implement robust security measures provided by Google Cloud Infrastructure, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
