Privacy Policy for Ramzan Prayer & Hadees

Last Updated: March 2024

Thank you for using Ramzan Prayer & Hadees. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information in our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Our app collects the following information:

  • Location Data: We access your device's location ONLY to provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. This data is processed locally on your device and NOT transmitted to our servers.
  • Device Information: Basic device info (model, Android version) for app functionality and bug fixing.
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate precise prayer times based on your location
  • To provide accurate Qibla direction
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To fix bugs and technical issues

3. Data Storage & Security

All sensitive data (like location) is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal data on external servers. The app works primarily offline.

4. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage data (you can opt-out)
  • No advertising networks are used in our app

5. Permissions

Our app requests these permissions:

  • Location: For prayer times and Qibla
  • Storage: To save Hadith data and settings
  • Internet: For app updates and fetching latest prayer time data

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
